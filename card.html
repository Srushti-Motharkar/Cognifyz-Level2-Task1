Manali(Himachal Pradesh)

Manali is a town, near Kullu town in Kullu district in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. It is situated in the northern end of the Kullu Valley, The town is located in the Kullu Delhi. district, approximately 270 kilometres north of the state capital of Shimla and northeast of the national capital of New Delhi

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands is a union territory of India It is grouped into two island groups: the northern Andaman Islands and the southern Nicobar Islands, Port Blair is the capital and largest city of the territory, located about 1,190 km from Chennai and 1,255 km from Kolkata in mainland India.

Shimla

Shimla also known as Simla, the official name until 1972) is the capital and the largest city of the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. In 1864, Shimla was declared as the summer capital of British India. After independence, the city became the capital of East Punjab and was later made the capital city of Himachal Pradesh

Matheran

Matheran is an automobile-free hill station and a municipal council in the Karjat taluka of the Raigad district located in the Indian state of Maharashtra. Matheran is part of the Mumbai Metropolitan Region. It is located in the Western Ghats, at an elevation of around 800 m (2,625 feet) above sea level.
